SpaceZ

❮ ❯

SpaceZ is a zombie game where you clear rooms of zombies using the backblast of your jetpack. Since there are no weapons in space, you must rely on your jetpack to survive.

Technical Details

Technologies used: Unity, C#, A* Pathfinding

Key Features

  • Infinite room generation with controlled room design
  • Seamless room transitions ensuring door alignment
  • Zombie AI using A* pathfinding

Challenges and Solutions

We worked with a culturally diverse group with varying skill levels, which sometimes led to communication challenges. We overcame this by using a project management tool to keep track of tasks and communicate effectively.
